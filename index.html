My major competences

Game Design

I specialize in game- and level design for emerging technologies, especially AR. I have a passion to work with merging physical and digital game design. I use tools and methods to analyze and adjust the gameplay experience according to player feedback.

Game Development

I have primarily worked with C#, C++, Python and Lua. I use Unity, Unreal, Maya, 3ds and Photoshop to facilitate my game development. I have also created board games using Arduinos and Raspberry Pies.

Spoken Level design, gameplay/AI scripting, Unity. October 2015 - December 2015 (Released).
Responsible for: Level design, environmental- and light design, AI scripting and gameplay programming (C#).

Ehran has been trapped in a dark dungeon. Here he meets the mysterious girl - Aya. The two do not share a language, but do share a desire for freedom! The game offers a unique opportunity for you to express yourself, creating and using your own personalized language, which only you and your companion understand. Exploring the dark and atmospheric dungeon, you encounter increasingly challenging puzzles, which require you to cooperate with Aya.
