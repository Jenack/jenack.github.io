My major competences

Game Design

I am a experienced Game and Level Designer who loves to create interactive experiences for emerging technologies. I have a passion for working with physical and digital game design in a local multiplayer or co-op setting. I use tools and methods to analyze and adjust the gameplay experience.

Development

I primarily develop games in C#, but I also work with C++, Python and Lua. Sometimes I dabble with frontend, which I used to create this website. I use Unity, Unreal, Maya, 3ds Max and Photoshop to facilitate my game development. I have also created board games using Arduinos and Raspberry Pi.
