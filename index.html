My major competences

Game Design

I am a experienced Game Designer who loves to create interactive experiences for emerging technologies, especially for AR/VR. I have a passion for working with physical and digital game design in a local multiplayer or co-op setting. I use tools and methods to analyze and adjust the gameplay experience.

Development

I have primarily developed games in C#, C++, Python and Lua. However, I also know HTML5, CSS3 and some JS, which I used to create this website. I use Unity, Unreal, Maya, 3ds Max and Photoshop to facilitate my game development. I have also created board games using Arduinos and Raspberry Pies.

Frantics Game design, level design, gameplay scripting in Unity. Still in development (TBA) by NapNok Games.

At NapNok I worked as a game designer on Frantics. I was responsible for two mini games, which I project managed and game designed.
Frantics is a PlayLink party-game where you and up to three friends face off in a variety of arcade-style mini-games hosted by a scheming, manipulative and... charmingly unfair Fox.

Nordic Game Jam Plays 2016 Project management, concept design and game design. April 8th 2016 - April 10th 2016.

For Nordic Game Jam 2016 I developed a concept of a multiplayer 2D platformer to play on Google Chromecast. Plays would go to NGJPlays.com, be assigned a random team color, the webserver would average players input and execute it.
I gathered a team of NGJ participants and helped create this interactive experience.

Spoken Level design, gameplay/AI scripting, Unity. October 2015 - December 2015.
Responsible for: Level design, environmental- and light design, AI scripting and gameplay programming (C#).

Ehran has been trapped in a dark dungeon. Here he meets the mysterious girl - Aya. The two do not share a language, but do share a desire for freedom! The game offers a unique opportunity for you to express yourself, creating and using your own personalized language, which only you and your companion understand.

Skyphant 2 - DADIU Minigame Level design and waypoint scripting in Unity. September 2015 - October 2015.

Skyphant 2 was developed in one week, by a team of 16 students from different educations, as a practice production before moving on to the exam production. As level designer I was responsible for creating the level and scripting the waypoint (C#) the elephant followed.

Skyphant - DADIU Minigame Level design, Unity. August 2015 - September 2015.

Skyphant was developed in one week, by a team of 16 students from different educations, as a practice production. As level designer I was responsible for creating the procedurally generated level.
